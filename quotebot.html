Description

Quotes inspire, motivate, and entertain us all, and The QuoteBot does much more than that. It CREATES its own quotes. With almost 8 BILLION possible quotes, you'll never run out of inspiration.

The QuoteBot is a quote-generating AI that will create an all-new quote at the tap of a button.

== FEATURES ==

- Generate 8 billion random quotes

- Bookmark your favorite quotes and save space

- Access Bookmarked quotes at any time

- Daily notifications with random quotes

- Easily share your favorite quotes with friends

== DAILY QUOTE NOTIFICATIONS (iOS 10+) ==

Turn on daily quote notifications to receive a notification every day with a new inspirational quote generated by The QuoteBot.

== ACCESSIBILITY ==

The QuoteBot is fully optimized with voiceover compatibility so everyone can enjoy The QuoteBot.

== QUOTEBOT PRO ==

Purchase QuoteBot Pro (via in-app purchase) to unlock several additional features to enhance your quote generating experience.

Pro features include:

- No ads

- Unlimited bookmarks

- Dark mode (great for nighttime quote generating)

- A back button to return to the previous quote
